

# Zazzle Salon – Thillainagar, Trichy | Premium Landing Page

## Overview
A high-converting, mobile-first single-page landing page for Zazzle Salon, a luxury unisex salon and bridal studio. Elegant gold/black/cream color palette reflecting premium positioning. The uploaded Zazzle logo will be used in the header and footer.

---

## Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Zazzle logo (from uploaded image) displayed top-left
- Minimal nav links: Services, Reviews, Contact
- "Book Now" CTA button in the header

### 2. Hero Section
- Full-width hero with luxurious salon interior placeholder image and dark overlay
- **Exact headline**: *"Luxury unisex salon and bridal studio in Thillainagar, delivering stylish hair, beauty and bridal experiences with premium ambience and expert care."*
- "Book via WhatsApp" prominent green CTA button
- Secondary CTA: "View Services"

### 3. Trust Bar
- Horizontal strip with **four** trust signals:
  - ⭐ 4.8 Average Rating
  - 5,300+ Ratings
  - 📸 1,400+ Client Photos
  - Established Since 2017
- Clean icon-based design with subtle animation on scroll

### 4. Service Architecture & Pricing Grid
- 5 service cards in a responsive grid:
  - **Haircuts & Styling** – Men ₹350, Women ₹500, Kids
  - **Hair Treatments & Colour** – Keratin ₹5,000, Colour ₹2,500
  - **Beauty & Skin** – Facials ₹1,999
  - **Bridal Packages** – Premium highlight card with gold accent
  - **Nails** – Manicures ₹599
- Each card: service icon, description, "starting from" pricing, WhatsApp booking link

### 5. Testimonials & Vibe
- Review carousel (auto-scroll + swipeable on mobile)
- 4-5 curated testimonials highlighting: professional staff, hygiene, bridal expertise
- Star ratings and reviewer names on each card

### 6. Lead Capture & Contact Form
- Simple appointment request form: Name, Phone, Service dropdown, Preferred Date

### 8. Sticky WhatsApp Button
- Floating WhatsApp button fixed to bottom-right

## Design Direction
- **Logo**: Uploaded Zazzle logo used in navbar and footer
- **Colors**: Deep black, gold accents (#C5A55A), cream/off-white backgrounds
- **Typography**: Elegant serif headings, clean sans-serif body
- **Mobile-first**: Optimized for fast local search conversions
- **No backend** – static page with WhatsApp links and client-side form
